Apple Butter Snickerdoodles

Be aware that these cookies may taste a bit different than what is expected, as they have a hint of apple butter.

I'd like to thank Kim for providing the recipe.

Ingredients

  • 2 ¾ cups all-purpose flour
  • ½ teaspoon cream of tartar
  • ½ teaspoon baking soda
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • ¼ teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • ¼ teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 1 ¾ cups white sugar, divided
  • ½ cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 large egg, at room temperature
  • ½ teaspoon vanilla extract
  • ¾ cup apple butter
  • 2 tablespoons ground cinnamon

Steps to making Apple Butter Snickerdoodles

  1. Step 1

    2. Whisk together flour, cream of tartar, baking soda, salt, 1/4 teaspoon cinnamon, and nutmeg in a medium bowl until combined.

  2. Step 2

    3. Whisk together flour, cream of tartar, baking soda, salt, 1/4 teaspoon cinnamon, and nutmeg in a medium bowl until combined.

  3. Step 3

    4. Whisk together flour, cream of tartar, baking soda, salt, 1/4 teaspoon cinnamon, and nutmeg in a medium bowl until combined.

  4. Step 4

    5. Mix together remaining 1/4 cup sugar and 2 tablespoons cinnamon in a shallow bowl or pie pan. Drop tablespoon-sized pieces of dough into the cinnamon-sugar mixture and roll into balls, coating well. Place 1 inch apart on the prepared baking sheets.

  5. Step 5

    6. Bake in the preheated oven until edges are just set, about 10 minutes. Allow cookies to cool on baking sheets for 5 minutes before removing to a wire rack to cool completely.

Cook's Note:

Use apple butter at room temperature.
